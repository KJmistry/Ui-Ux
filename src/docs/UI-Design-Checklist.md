# UI Design Checklist (Minimal Reference)

## 1. Visual Style & Aesthetics
- Glassmorphism  
  - Purpose: Create depth and modern, premium feel
- Neumorphism  
  - Purpose: Soft, tactile UI elements
- Flat Design  
  - Purpose: Clarity, simplicity, scalability
- Material Design  
  - Purpose: Consistent behavior and usability
- Minimalism  
  - Purpose: Focus on essential content
- Skeuomorphism  
  - Purpose: Familiarity through real-world metaphors
- Brutalism  
  - Purpose: Experimental, bold expression
- Claymorphism  
  - Purpose: Friendly, playful interfaces

## 2. Layout & Structure
- Grid System  
  - Purpose: Alignment and consistency
- Spacing & Whitespace  
  - Purpose: Readability and visual balance
- Visual Hierarchy  
  - Purpose: Guide user attention
- Responsive Layout  
  - Purpose: Multi-device adaptability

## 3. Color & Contrast
- Color Palette  
  - Purpose: Brand identity and mood
- Contrast Ratios  
  - Purpose: Accessibility and legibility
- Light / Dark Mode  
  - Purpose: Comfort and preference support
- State Colors (success, error, warning)  
  - Purpose: Feedback and clarity

## 4. Typography
- Font Family Selection  
  - Purpose: Tone and readability
- Font Scale  
  - Purpose: Hierarchy and scanning
- Line Height & Letter Spacing  
  - Purpose: Text clarity
- Text Contrast  
  - Purpose: Accessibility

## 5. Components & Controls
- Buttons  
  - Purpose: Primary actions
- Inputs & Forms  
  - Purpose: Data entry
- Cards & Containers  
  - Purpose: Group related content
- Modals & Drawers  
  - Purpose: Focused interactions
- Navigation (navbar, tabs, sidebar)  
  - Purpose: Wayfinding

## 6. Interaction & Feedback
- Hover States  
  - Purpose: Affordance indication
- Active / Pressed States  
  - Purpose: Action confirmation
- Focus States  
  - Purpose: Keyboard accessibility
- Loading States  
  - Purpose: System feedback
- Empty States  
  - Purpose: User guidance

## 7. Motion & Animation
- Micro-interactions  
  - Purpose: Enhance usability
- Transitions  
  - Purpose: Smooth context changes
- Animation Timing  
  - Purpose: Natural feel
- Motion Restraint  
  - Purpose: Avoid distraction

## 8. Accessibility
- Keyboard Navigation  
  - Purpose: Non-mouse usability
- Screen Reader Support  
  - Purpose: Inclusive access
- Touch Target Size  
  - Purpose: Mobile usability
- Color Blind Safety  
  - Purpose: Inclusive design

## 9. Performance & Practicality
- Visual Effect Cost  
  - Purpose: Maintain performance
- Asset Optimization  
  - Purpose: Faster load times
- Consistency Across Screens  
  - Purpose: Predictability
- Design System Alignment  
  - Purpose: Scalability and maintenance

## 10. Brand & Product Context
- Brand Alignment  
  - Purpose: Identity consistency
- Target Audience Fit  
  - Purpose: Appropriate tone
- Platform Guidelines  
  - Purpose: Native user expectations
